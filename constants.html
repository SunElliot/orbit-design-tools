📖 How to use

  1. All OrbitTools calculators use exactly these constants, so results are consistent across tools.OrbitTools 所有计算器均使用本页常数，各工具结果保持一致。
  2. Use the typical-orbit rows as starting points, then refine altitude/inclination in the design tools.典型轨道各行可作为设计起点，再用各设计工具细化高度/倾角。

Notes

Values follow WGS-84 / EGM-96 conventions as commonly used in mission analysis (Vallado, SMAD). The critical inclination and the sun-rate are exact consequences of the J2 secular equations — see the J2 Perturbations tool. GEO altitude is quoted above the equator; the graveyard offset follows the IADC formula for typical spacecraft (≈ 235 km + area-to-mass term).

说明

数值采用任务分析常用的 WGS-84 / EGM-96 约定（Vallado、SMAD）。临界倾角与太阳速率由 J2 长期方程精确导出——见 J2 摄动工具。GEO 高度为赤道上空值；坟墓轨道抬高量按 IADC 公式（≈ 235 km + 面质比项）。
