Beta angle and eclipse model

The beta angle is the angle between the sun vector and the orbit plane:

sin β = cos δ_s·sin i·sin(Ω − α_s) + sin δ_s·cos i

with (α_s, δ_s) the sun's right ascension/declination from a low-precision ephemeris. For a circular orbit in a cylindrical Earth shadow, an eclipse occurs when |β| < β* where sin β* = R_E/(R_E+h), and the eclipse fraction is

f_E = (1/π)·acos( √(h²+2·R_E·h) / ((R_E+h)·cos β) )

The year sweep advances Ω at the J2 secular rate (sun rate for SSO) and the sun along the ecliptic. Penumbra and oblateness effects (~±1–2 min) are neglected — adequate for power/thermal sizing.

β 角与阴影模型

β 角为太阳矢量与轨道面的夹角：

sin β = cos δ_s·sin i·sin(Ω − α_s) + sin δ_s·cos i

其中 (α_s, δ_s) 为低精度星历给出的太阳赤经/赤纬。圆轨道、圆柱形地影假设下，当 |β| < β*（sin β* = R_E/(R_E+h)）时出现阴影，阴影比例为

f_E = (1/π)·acos( √(h²+2·R_E·h) / ((R_E+h)·cos β) )

全年推演中 Ω 按 J2 长期速率（SSO 按太阳速率）推进，太阳沿黄道运动。忽略半影与地球扁率影响（约 ±1–2 分钟），满足能源/热设计精度。
